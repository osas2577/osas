What We Do

We’re always looking for new faces and fresh ideas

ANC

Obio Cottage Hospital provides exceptional Antenatal Care (ANC) services, offering personalized support, comprehensive health check-ups, and valuable guidance for expectant mothers. Your journey to motherhood begins with us.

Immunization

Prioritizes your child's well-being with top-notch Immunization services. Our expert team ensures a seamless and safe experience, safeguarding your little one's health from the start.

Out Patient Clinic

Convenient and accessible, our outpatient services cater to individuals who do not require overnight stays. Receive expert care without disrupting your daily routine.

Inpatient Clinic

For patients requiring overnight stays, our inpatient services ensure personalized care and attention in a comfortable environment. Our dedicated staff is here to support your recovery.

Prevention Of Mother To Child Transmission Of HIV (PMTCT)

our commitment to health extends to preventing Mother-to-Child Transmission of HIV (PMTCT). Trust us for expert care, ensuring a safe and healthy beginning for both mother and child.

Ultra Sound Scan

Our state-of-the-art diagnostic services include advanced imaging and laboratory tests. Accurate and timely diagnostics are crucial for effective healthcare.

Family Planning

Take control of your reproductive health with the Family Planning Services at Obio Cottage Hospital. Our compassionate and confidential family planning clinic provides a range of services, including contraceptive counseling, family planning education, and access to various birth control methods. Our goal is to empower individuals and families to make informed decisions about their reproductive health, ensuring that your choices align with your personal goals and preferences.

Cervical Cancer Screen

Discover comprehensive Women's Health Services at our facility, featuring crucial Cervical Cancer Screening. Safeguard your health proactively with our expert care, ensuring early detection and personalized prevention for a confident and healthy future.

Dietician Clinic

Nourish your body and cultivate a healthier lifestyle with the Dietician Clinic at Obio Cottage Hospital. Our registered dieticians are here to offer expert guidance on nutrition, weight management, and overall wellness. Whether you're seeking advice for a specific health condition, aiming for weight loss, or looking to adopt a balanced diet, our team will work with you to create a customized plan that aligns with your goals. Embrace a healthier you with the support and expertise of our dedicated dieticians.

Physician Clinic

Physician Clinic at Obio Cottage Hospital, where your health and well-being are our top priorities. Our team of experienced and compassionate physicians is dedicated to providing comprehensive medical care for patients of all ages. From routine check-ups to managing chronic conditions, we are here to address your healthcare needs with personalized attention and expertise. Trust us to guide you on your journey to optimal health and vitality.

Optical Clinic

Thriving Through Life: Tailored Healthcare at Every Stage

  1. Management is committed to the continuous improvement in the level of care available at Obio Cottage Hospital and as such has setup up a quality improvement and patient safety program to ensure that clients receive a standard of care that is safe, acceptable and affordable
  2. Our focus is on the integration of health systems components to bring about the safest, most effective and efficient level of health care available
  3. A strong emphasis is laid on building and developing capacity of care providers in the areas of clinical care, quality improvement and risk management to ensure quality care is provided
  4. We recognize that quality of care speaks to not only clinical competence but also, meeting the needs and requirements of our clients. Client satisfaction is at the heart of the quality improvement program. The Client Relations office is setup to attend to client’s grievances and complaints and mediate resolution. Do you have a complaint or feedback about your experience at OCH? Please let us know.
  5. The quality Improvement program spans the entire hospital looking at infection control practices, risk management practices as well as patient safety. Were there any errors in your care and management? Please let us know.
  6. Incident and accident reporting;In order to promote learning from experiences we encourage all staff and clients to report all accidents; e.g. falls, slips, needle stick injuries, medication errors.
